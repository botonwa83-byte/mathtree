# 隐私政策 · Privacy Policy

**应用名称 / App：APEX · 数学制高点（MathTree）**
**开发者 / Developer：Top King**
**最近更新 / Last updated：2026-06-12**

## 中文版

感谢你使用「APEX · 数学制高点」。我们高度重视你的隐私。本应用的设计原则是：**所有数据只保存在你的设备本地，我们不收集、不上传、不共享任何个人信息。**

### 1. 我们收集哪些信息
**我们不向服务器收集任何个人信息。** 登录为可选功能：你可以输入手机号作为本地学习 ID（仅保存在你设备的系统钥匙串中，不上传、无短信验证、无服务器账户），也可以选择游客模式直接使用全部学习功能。除此之外，应用不索取你的姓名、邮箱、位置、通讯录、相册、麦克风、摄像头等任何权限或个人数据。

### 2. 本地存储的内容
为实现学习功能，应用会在你的设备本地保存以下数据：
- 手机号（如选择登录；存于系统钥匙串 Keychain）；
- 做题记录、答题对错、错题本；
- 复习计划、连续学习天数、成就进度；
- 题目掌握度等学习统计。

这些数据**仅存储于你的设备本地**，不会发送到任何服务器，我们也无法访问。卸载应用即会清除这些数据。

### 2.1 账户删除
你可以随时在「更多 → 我的 → 删除账户」中**永久删除账户**：账户及关联的手机号会立即从设备钥匙串中清除，不可恢复。由于账户数据从不上传，本地删除即为完全删除。

### 3. 网络与第三方
- 本应用为**纯离线应用**，公式渲染所需的 KaTeX 组件已内置于应用内，正常使用无需联网。
- 本应用**不接入任何第三方广告、统计、分析或追踪 SDK**。
- 本应用**不进行用户追踪（App Tracking Transparency 中声明为不追踪）**。

### 4. 儿童隐私
本应用面向高考数学学习者，内容适合各年龄段，且不收集任何个人信息，因此不存在儿童个人信息相关风险。

### 5. 数据安全
由于不收集、不上传任何数据，你的学习数据始终在你自己的设备上，安全由 iOS 系统保障。

### 6. 政策变更
如本政策有更新，我们会在应用内或本页面公布最新版本。

### 7. 联系我们
如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 App Store 的开发者支持页面联系开发者 Top King。

## English Version

Thank you for using **APEX · MathTree**. We take your privacy seriously. The design principle of this app is: **all data is stored only locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or share any personal information.**

### 1. Information We Collect
**We collect no personal information on any server.** Sign-in is optional: you may enter a phone number as a local study ID (stored only in your device's system Keychain — never uploaded, no SMS verification, no server-side account), or use guest mode with full access to all learning features. Beyond that, the app does not request your name, email, location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or any other permission or personal data.

### 2. Data Stored Locally
To provide learning features, the app stores the following data locally on your device:
- your phone number (only if you choose to sign in; stored in the system Keychain);
- practice records, right/wrong answers, mistake book;
- review schedule, study streak, achievements;
- per-problem mastery statistics.

This data **never leaves your device**, is never sent to any server, and is inaccessible to us. Deleting the app removes this data.

### 2.1 Account Deletion
You can **permanently delete your account** at any time in "More → Profile → Delete Account". The account and its associated phone number are immediately removed from the device Keychain and cannot be recovered. Since account data is never uploaded, local deletion is complete deletion.

### 3. Network & Third Parties
- The app is **fully offline**. The KaTeX component used to render formulas is **bundled inside the app**; normal use requires no internet.
- The app contains **no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs**.
- The app **does not track users** (declared as "Not Tracking" under App Tracking Transparency).

### 4. Children's Privacy
The app is intended for math learners and is suitable for all ages. Since it collects no personal information, there is no risk to children's personal data.

### 5. Data Security
Because no data is collected or uploaded, your study data always remains on your own device, protected by iOS.

### 6. Changes
If this policy changes, we will publish the updated version in the app or on this page.

### 7. Contact
For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer **Top King** via the developer support page on the App Store.
